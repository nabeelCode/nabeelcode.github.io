Our Company

Vanes Business Corporation, is an air conditioning contracting company, with diversified organization for consultation, designing, estimation, contracting, sub contracting and maintenance services, operating all over Northern Kerala. We pride ourselves in providing first class turnkey services in Industry. Our thrust is to deliver with highest regard to Quality, Integrity, Honesty and Customer satisfaction. Vanes continues to enjoy strong and ongoing beneficial relationship with a number of respected companies and customers including substantial repeated business throughout the years. We are basically from Calicut, having customer backup in all the areas in Northern Kerala. We are growing and obtained a remarkable position in market by working with the idea that every client is a long term relationship. We have earned the reputation of being the experience professionals, which makes us to enable with all manner of project situations. We can help you achieve your objectives of time, cost and performance.
